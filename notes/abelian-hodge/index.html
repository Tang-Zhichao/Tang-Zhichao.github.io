Notes on the Hodge conjecture of abelian varieties
Posted on 2025-06-10:: abelian variety Hodge conjecture

In spring of 2025, the topic of the learning seminar is the recent work of Markman on Hodge conjecture of abelian varieties. In particular, he obtains the full Hodge conjecture for abelian varieties of dimension $4$.

Here is some notes on several sections of this work, which is covered by myself and contains the main prototypes and examples.
